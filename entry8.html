Old T Shirt

This shirt used to belong to my oldest cousin, Conner, and then my brother, Anthony. Conner used to be on the Lacrosse team in high school and was able to go to college on a lacrosse scholarship. Once he no longer needed the shirt, he cut the sleeves off and used it as a workout shirt. When he passed it down to Anthony, Anthony also used it as a workout shirt. This item is significant because it reminds me of my oldest cousin who has always been an inspiration to all the cousins and who is now a covid frontline worker, something the entire family is very proud of.

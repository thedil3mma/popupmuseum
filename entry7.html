Nana Carol's Earrings

Whenever I go home to San Antonio, Texas, my mom makes an effort to spend time with my “Nana Carol.” Although my “Nana Carol” is not biologically related to me, she holds a very important place in my life, and we share a series of valuable family rituals that always make me feel at home. Every time we visit Nana Carol, we eat, shop, gossip, and before we leave, my Nana Carol gives my mom and me bags full of face creams she tried and didn't like and old shiny jewelry. My mom and I always laugh about the things Nana Carol gives us, but every now and then we find some really amazing new face wash or a beautiful pair of earrings like these. I don't wear the earrings very often, but I keep them in the main part of my jewelry box and they always remind me of my Nana Carol.

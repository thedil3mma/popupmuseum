Grandfather's Box

This is a hand carved wooden box that my grandpa bought once when he traveled to Thailand, his favorite place to visit in the world. I inherited it when he passed away, and I have kept it with me for years because it actually has a little key that goes with it that locks it shut very well. Recently my cat knocked it off of my desk and it lost one of its little legs, but I eventually plan on gluing it back so that shouldn’t be an issue. I can’t explain why, but it holds a lot of significance to me and has become a staple in every room I have, whether in college or at home.

Family Ring

This ring belonged to my mother before it belonged to me. It was given to her by her mother when she was twenty-one, and last year, when I was twenty-two, my mother passed it on to me. My mother kept this ring through multiple moves. From Boston to Florence to Madrid to New York City, and finally back to Massachusetts. Before my mom wore this ring, my grandma did. Neither of us knows where she got it, but she too had it quite some time. I was never close with my grandmother, but wearing it or simply keeping it in my room provides me with a connection I no longer have in person. My family isn’t very sentimental and this is one of the only objects I have that connects me and my mother and grandmother as a continuous family line, even if the object itself isn’t in the best condition.

Father's T-Shirt

This is a t-shirt that used to be my dad’s, and on the shirt are the four horsemen of Notre Dame where my grandfather went to college. My dad loves this shirt because it reminds him of his dad, but now it’s been passed down to me which makes it even more special. I don’t know where it was made but probably a factory. My dad got it from his dad and then I got it from my dad. I came to own the item because I like to sleep in my dad’s giant t-shirts. I love this item because it makes me feel more connected to my family.
